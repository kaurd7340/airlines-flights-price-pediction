Airlines Flights Analysis

End-to-end airline analytics project focused on ticket pricing, travel patterns, route performance, airline revenue analysis and operational insights using Power BI dashboards and business intelligence techniques.

Explore Dashboards View Insights

Problem Statement

The airline industry handles large volumes of travel and pricing data daily. Airlines require analytical insights to understand passenger travel patterns, route performance, pricing behavior and operational efficiency. The objective of this project is to perform exploratory data analysis on airline travel data and identify meaningful trends, patterns and business insights related to airline operations and fare distribution.

Key Business Insights

Revenue Insights

  • Jet Airways generated the highest revenue among all airlines.
  • Total airline revenue crossed ₹97 Million.
  • Premium travel classes generated higher ticket revenue.
  • Revenue concentration was observed among a small number of major airlines.

Route Insights

  • DEL → BOM → COK emerged as the most popular route.
  • Delhi was the most frequent source city.
  • Cochin was the most common destination city.
  • Certain routes consistently generated higher average ticket prices.

Pricing Insights

  • Average ticket price remained around ₹9.09K.
  • Highest observed ticket fare reached ₹80K.
  • Business class tickets showed significantly higher pricing.
  • Ticket prices varied strongly depending on route and duration.

Operational Insights

  • Average travel duration remained close to 11 hours.
  • Airline traffic concentrated around major metro cities.
  • Route frequency and travel demand showed strong correlation.
  • Power BI helped uncover route-level operational patterns.
